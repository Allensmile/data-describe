Get started with data-describe
data-describe is a Python toolkit for inspecting, illuminating, and investigating enormous amounts of unknown data with mixed relationships.

With unknown “dark” data, “unclean” data, structured and unstructured data, and data embedded in images and documents, it can be difficult to get a clear understanding of your data environment. data-describe profiles the data and reveals the true landscape of all of your data. This toolset provides a Data Scientist a rich set of tools chained together to automate common [tasks]. These insights help facilitate conversations among other data scientists, engineers, and business analysts, ultimately lending itself to future innovation. data-describe was built by the same [team] that leads Tensorflow, XGboost, Kubeflow, and MXNet, who have combined over 40 years of Data Science Experience.
